Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register to vote?

Only eligible students, faculty, and staff of Cavite State University with institutional email accounts can register.

Is my vote secure and confidential?

Yes. eBalota uses secure authentication and encryption to protect voter privacy and prevent multiple voting.

Can I vote from multiple devices?

You can log in from different devices, but the system only allows one valid vote per user for each election.

How can I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page to reset your password via your institutional email.

About eBalota

eBalota is Cavite State University's secure and transparent web-based voting system designed to modernize the election process for students, faculty, and staff. Our platform ensures that every eligible voter can participate safely and conveniently using their institutional email accounts for verification.

Built with security, flexibility, and transparency as our core priorities, eBalota supports a seamless voting experience that respects voter privacy while providing real-time election results and analytics to promote trust and accountability within the university community.

Through eBalota, Cavite State University continues its commitment to innovation and excellence, empowering the CvSU community to make their voices heard in an efficient, accessible, and trustworthy manner.
