Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register to vote?

Only eligible students, faculty, and staff of CvSU with institutional email accounts can register.

Is my vote secure and confidential?

Yes, the system uses secure authentication and encryption to protect voter privacy.

Can I vote from multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in from different devices, but only one vote per election is allowed per user.

How can I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via your institutional email.

About eVOTE

eVOTE is Cavite State University's secure and transparent web-based voting system designed to modernize the election process for students, faculty, and staff. Our platform ensures that every eligible voter can participate safely and conveniently using their institutional email accounts for verification.

Built with security, flexibility, and transparency as our core priorities, eVOTE supports a seamless voting experience that respects voter privacy while providing real-time election results and analytics to promote trust and accountability within the university community.

Through eVOTE, Cavite State University continues its commitment to innovation and excellence, empowering the community to make their voices heard in an efficient and trustworthy manner.
